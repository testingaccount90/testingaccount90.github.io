Executive Summary

SO&E Education & Translation Services is a language services business offering interpretation, translation, online teaching, video tutorials, and academic support. We specialize in English-Albanian and English-Italian language pairs, providing high-quality, accessible solutions for individuals and small groups.

Mission: To bridge communication and learning gaps through accurate translation and personalized education services.

Vision: To become a trusted provider of bilingual education and translation services in our region and expand globally via online platforms.

Services Offered

Interpretation Services

Face-to-face interpretation for personal, academic, and business needs.

Written Translation

Professional English ⇄ Albanian, English ⇄ Italian document translation.

Online Teaching

1-on-1 and group English language lessons via Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet.

Video Tutorials

Pre-recorded English grammar videos explaining rules and usage with clarity.

Academic Support

Assistance with school homework, exercises, and language-related assignments.

Target Market

  • Immigrants and expats in need of language support.
  • Students seeking English language tutoring or schoolwork help.
  • Professionals needing business translation services.
  • Parents looking for supplemental education for their children.
