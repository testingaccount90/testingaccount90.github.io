About Us

At SO&E, we specialize in bridging language and learning gaps through personalized education and professional translation services. With a focus on English-Albanian and English-Italian, we support students, professionals, and families to thrive in a multilingual world.

Mission: To bridge communication and learning gaps through accurate translation and personalized education services.

Vision: To become a trusted provider of bilingual education and translation services locally and globally.
